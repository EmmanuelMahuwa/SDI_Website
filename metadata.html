Properties

Summary:
This dataset contains land parcel polygons representing properties within a defined study area in the City of Cape Town. The data was derived by clipping the official “Land Parcels” feature class from the City of Cape Town’s Geoportal. Each parcel includes associated cadastral and property information.

Study Area – Big Bay Suburb

Summary:
This dataset defines the spatial extent of a study area based on the outer boundary (extent) of properties extracted from the City of Cape Town’s official Land Parcels feature class. The study area was created by georeferencing corner vertices around the clipped properties.
